The 3Xs

Membership: 1X, 2X, 3X

Purpose: To fight crime.

Affiliations: The Commissioner

Enemies: Green Terror (said to be African, although he is drawn as an aged golden-skinned Caucasian, blood-drinker, allegedly immortal).

Base of Operations: New York

First Appearance: Mystic Comics #1 (Timely Comics, March 1940)

History: The 3X's were a trio of crime-fighting siblings, each of whom brought specific abilities to their endeavours - 1X was the detective, leaning more to Sam Spade than Sherlock Holmes; 2X was the brains, a knowledgeable bald genius who carried a disintegration gun; and 3X was the brawn, a hulking brute of a man.

In their only recorded case, the 3X's investigated a kidnapping which eventually brought them into conflict with the blood-drinking Green Terror and his gang.

Comments: The 3X's made their sole appearance in Mystic Comics #1, and haven't been seen since. They were probably based on a similar group who starred in the popular U.S. radio show "I Love A Mystery".
