The Fourth Generation of Morituri

Membership: Unknown

Purpose: prior to mutation To fight the Horde; after mutation to die and end their misery

Affiliations: None

Enemies: None

Base of Operations: Unknown

First Appearance: Strikeforce: Morituri #17 (Marvel Comics)

History: An attempt to create supersoldiers using the Morituri process without the involvement of Dr.Tuolema, the scientist who had developed it, several volunteers were hideously transformed into genetic monstrosities when things went wrong. Soon after the other Morituri discovered them, the victims committed suicide to end their pain.

Comments: Created by Carl Potts.
