Sazan Eyes (3x3 Eyes)

Created, written and drawn by Yuzo Takada, Sazan Eyes is your everyday story of indestructible teenage zombies protecting ancient three-eyed beings from demons of the Shadow World. First appearing in Young Magazine (a weekly title published by Kodansha), Sazan Eyes (a.k.a. 3 x 3 Eyes) is the story of Pai, an immortal Sanjiyan, of Fujii Yajumo, the school boy whose soul she took, turning him into her invulnerable undead protector, and of Pai's quest to become human. Published in English by Innovation, and latterly Dark Horse, the Manga has also been turned anime by Bandai Visual, who have released several installments of the series in that format.
