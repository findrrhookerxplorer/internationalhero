Wonder Man

Real Name: Fred Carson

Identity/Class: Human empowered by magic ring

Occupation: Radio engineer, inventor

Affiliations: Brenda Hastings

Enemies: General Attilla

Known Relatives:

Aliases:

Base of Operations:

First Appearance: Wonder Comics #1 (Fox, May 1939)

Powers/Abilities: So long as he wears his magic ring, Wonder Man has superhuman strength (the strongest man on Earth) and is invulnerable. Also able to leap thousands of feet.

History: Given a magic ring by a strange benefactor during a visit to Tibet, Fred Carson swore to forever be the champion of the oppressed, defender of the weak, and relentless foe of all that is evil and unjust.

Comments: Created by Will Eisner.
