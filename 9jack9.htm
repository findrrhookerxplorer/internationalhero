9-JACK-9

Real Name: Sir John Sheers; J9AC9K

Identity/Class: Electronic lifeform, formerly human mutant

Occupation: Assassin

Affiliations: None

Enemies: Zot

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Mobile throughout the galaxy

First Appearance: Zot! #2 (Eclipse, 1984)

Powers/Abilities: Able to travel through and possess any form of electronic device or signal.

History: For amusement Sir John Sheers used his ability to astrally project to become the virtual reality killer 9-Jack-9, the universe's deadliest assassin. For decades he plied his trade, until eventually the hero Zot discovered his true identity. Though Sheers died in the confrontation, 9-Jack-9 lived on, taking independent life.

Comments: Created by Scott McCloud

CLARIFICATIONS: None.
