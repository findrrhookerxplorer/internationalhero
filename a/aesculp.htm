Aesculpian

Real Name: Margie

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: former Canadian Army surgeon.

Affiliations: Member of the Potentate

Enemies: Lucifer, Black Day, The Damned

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: Aes

Base of Operations: Unknown

First Appearance: The Watch, Volume 2, #1 (Phosphorescent Comics, September 2001)

Powers/Abilities: Aesculpian can empathise with and control organic matter at a range of several metres. She can use this to animate organic material to follow her commands, and to stimulate growth or healing.

History: Aesculpian  is a member of the Potentate, one of the world's most powerful super teams, and certainly the most radically active. They respect no political boundaries and go where they feel they are most needed, regardless of what an authority group thinks of it. Although Aesculpian can use her powers offensively, she is more commonly used for intelligence gathering (her powers can pinpoint people and their exact positions from a distance and without seeing them), and healing after the fight ends.

Comments: Created by Christian Read and Andrew Phillips

CLARIFICATIONS: None
