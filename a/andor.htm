Andor

Real Name: Unrevealed

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: Wanderer, former steelworker, weapon

Affiliations: Iron Maiden, formerly Lord Uru

Enemies: Lord Uru, SPIDER, formerly NoMan, Dynamo, Lightning

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: The Amazing Andor

Base of Operations: Mobile

First Appearance: Dynamo #1 (Tower Comics, August 1966)

Powers/Abilities: Incredible strength and agility, rapid healing, and telekinesis.

History: Kidnapped by an underground race of aliens as a baby, Andor was raised under alien technology to be turned into a weapon meant to destroy the human race. One thing changed his mind: a T.H.U.N.D.E.R. agent named Kitty Kane. Originally hating the human race, Andor's point of view was changed when he first layed eyes on the beautiful female. Unsure of where his loyalties were due, Andor attacked his former masters, and became an outcast of both races. The current whereabouts of Andor are unknown.

Comments: Thanks to Mac Murdock for informing me of this character's existence, and for providing information and images of him. Both he and Josh Geren have noted that he is unique because he was the first character to have a healing factor that was noted in the context of the story.

CLARIFICATIONS: None
