Actionfolkwife

Real Name: Helen Acosta

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: Community college instructor

Affiliations: Actionfolksinger, Captain Bubble, the Phenomes

Enemies: Strong Arm

Known Relatives: Norman "Bud" Fricker (grandfather, deceased, a.k.a. Sgt.Fricker), Norm Junior (father, a.k.a. Sgt. Fricker Jr.), Lucille Fricker (mother), Carrie (sister), Matie (sister, a.k.a. Capt. Bubble), Enrique Acosta (husband, a.k.a. Actionfolksinger)

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Bakersfield

First Appearance: Actionfolksinger#3

Powers/Abilities: Helen has the ability to discharge large amounts of electrical energy.

History: Helen comes from a long line of heroes. In the course of their adventures her parents and grand parents where exposed to a lot of strange energies. As a result the children in her generation where all born with special powers. Helen had no desire to lead a life of adventure and so kept her head down, earned her degree, got married and settled into a peaceful life. Unfortunately she married Enrique Acosta who was at one time a super hero sidekick. When his identity was exposed via the Internet he faced semi regular attacks from super villains. It was during a confrontation with StrongArm that Helen first showed her husband her super powers. She remains a staunch ally and defender of her husband.

Comments: Created by Enrique Acosta, who also supplied the above profile.
