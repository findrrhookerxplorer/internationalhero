Albrecht Strong

Real Name: Albrecht Strong

Identity/Class: Human mutant

Occupation: Would-be world conqueror

Affiliations: Ingrid Weiss

Enemies: Tom Strong; Tesla Strong

Known Relatives: Tom Strong (father); Ingrid Weiss (mother); Tesla Strong (half-sister), Paul Saveen (uncle)

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Millennium City

First Appearance: Tom Strong

Powers/Abilities: Superhuman strength and durability.

History: Albrecht Strong is the son of the "science hero" Tom Strong, although for many years his father was unaware of his existence. His mother is a Nazi superwoman, Ingrid Weiss, who captured Tom during the last days of World War II. While Tom was unconscious, a sample of his seed was extracted without his knowledge, and later used to impregnate Ingrid. Her son from this process, Albrecht, had his father's superhuman strength and durability, but was brought up with his mother's Aryan ideals.

Comments: Created by Alan Moore.
