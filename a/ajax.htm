Ajax

Real Name: Jim Wilson

Identity/Class: Extra-terrestrial being

Occupation: Policeman

Affiliations: Unknown

Enemies: Unknown

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: The Sun Man, Jim Wilson

Base of Operations: Unknown
formerly the Sun

First Appearance: Doc Savage #2 (Street and Smith, 1940)

Powers/Abilities: Superhuman strength, able to fly, able to generate intense heat, and immune to same.

History: Ajax came to Earth from the heart of the sun, and stayed to become a crime fighter, adopting the secret identity of police officer Jim Wilson.

Comments: After three years of adventures in the 1940s, Ajax was revived in modern time to guest star in Femforce.

CLARIFICATIONS: None.
