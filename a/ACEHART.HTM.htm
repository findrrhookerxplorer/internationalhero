Ace Hart

Real Name: Ace Hart (apparently)

Identity/Class: Human mutate (?)

Occupation: Scientist; adventurer

Affiliations: Val Venture

Enemies: Zonda

Known Relatives: None known

Aliases: The Atom Man

Base of Operations: Unknown

First Appearance: Super Thriller #6 (Foldes, 1948)

Powers/Abilities: Superstrong and "able to fly faster than a jet" (fast enough to cover a hundred miles in a couple of minutes). He can fly unaided and unprotected through interstellar space, covering distance such as the Earth to the Moon in minutes. His invulnerability not only has bullets bounce off but also energy rays from aliens pistols; he can also withstand the cold and pressures from being in outer space. He can emit "atomic heat rays" from his body, capable of melting metal.

History: "Ace Hart, a young scientist, has been able harness atomic energy to his own body, which gives him the strength of twenty men, and enables him to fly faster than a jet." He operates from a laboratory which has a device for sensing when and where people are in danger, so that he can race to their rescue.

Comments: One of his first enemies was Zonda, a.k.a. Satan's Daughter. He was later on partnered with Val Venture.

Ace reappeared as a Lliogor host body in Zenith Phase 3.

Thanks to Dennis Ray for additional information, most notably expanding greatly on Ace's powers.
