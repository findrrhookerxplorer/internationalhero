Aguiluz

Real Name: Aguiluz

Identity/Class: Magical being (Mulawin)

Occupation: Protector

Affiliations: Alwina, other Mulawin (Bagwis, Dakila, Aviona,

Enemies: Ravenas (Rasmus, Vultura, Kuwak, Tuka); Habagat (Mulawin traitor)

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Philippines

First Appearance: Mulawin (GMA 7, 2004)

Powers/Abilities: Able to fly, other powers unknown.

History: The Mulawin are a race of magical beings, part man and part bird, who serve to defend humanity from the Ravenas, a subversive sub-set of their own species. Years ago Bagwis, one of the senior Mulawin, fathered a child with his human lover, Veronica; he hid his daughter Alwina to grow up among the humans, secretly defended by Aguiluz, a young but capable Mulawin. Now that Alwina is fully grown, Aguiluz has fallen in love with her, and is forced to constantly battle the Ravenas, as they seek to destroy both Alwina and the entire human race.

Comments: Played by Richard Gutierrez. Mulawin is an ongoing series, and currently one of the Philippines' top soaps. The show's Official site is here.
