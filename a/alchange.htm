Al Change

Real Name: Al Change (or perhaps Jimmy Green - see Notes)

Identity/Class: Normal human

Occupation: School boy

Affiliations: None

Enemies: Unknown

Known Relatives: Unnamed father (an actor)

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Unknown.

First Appearance: Topper #1440 (D.C.Thomson, 6 September 1980)

Powers/Abilities: Expert disguise artist.

History: Al Change is the son of an actor, and seems to have inherited a skill for disguise.

Comments: There was an identical character with the same first adventure in a text story in issue one of the Beano (30 July 1938) - only this character was called Jimmy Green.

CLARIFICATIONS: None.
