Kai

Real Name: Kai

Identity/Class: Normal human, able to use ki energy to perform superhuman feats

Occupation: Assassin

Affiliations: SIVA (S-IV [e.g. S-4] the School of Secret Slaying Skills.)

Enemies: Angel Ace

Known Relatives: None known

Aliases: None known

Base of Operations: Philippines

First Appearance: Angel Ace (Alamat Comics)

Powers/Abilities: Incredibly deadly martial artist, with her skills heightened by use of ki energy. This allows her to perform superhuman feats, running miles without tiring, leaping great heights from a standing start, resisting injury, or causing devastating damage with her bare hands or feet. Highly acrobatic. Trained  amongst many other skills to be a master of disguise, to resist pain and to control her own breathing.

History: Kai is a youthful assassin who was charged with killing Angel Ace. Once a friend to Angel, she is now her most dangerous adversary.

Comments: Created by Marco Dimaano

CLARIFICATIONS: None
