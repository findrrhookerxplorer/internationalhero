Adhemar

Real Name: Adhemar

Identity/Class: Normal human (?)

Occupation: Former university lecturer, former government minister

Affiliations: Unknown

Enemies: Unknown

Known Relatives: Nero (father); "Madame" (mother)

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Belgium

First Appearance: Nero newspaper strip

Powers/Abilities: Adhemar is a genius, with a penchant for invention.

History: Adhemar is so intelligent that he started speaking the day he was born, and taught at both Oxford and Cambridge by the time he was three.
