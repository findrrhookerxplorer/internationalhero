Batch 72

Thirty years ago a genetic experiment was carried out by a secret organisation. They used the Philippines to test a DNA altering drug called Batch 72. The results were beyond their wildest dreams, with one in a hundred exposed gaining superhuman powers. After that it was difficult to keep the experiment a secret, and in the early 1980's the organisation's operations were publically exposed and discontinued.

Nowadays thousands of Filipinos are "Super Pinoys". One group, all college students in Manila, decide to form a garage band called Batch 72. They don't plan to be superheroes, but life sometimes refuses to go to plan...
