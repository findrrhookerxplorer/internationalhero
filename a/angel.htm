Angel

Real Name: Harry Angel

Identity/Class: Cyborg

Occupation: Fighter pilot

Affiliations: RAF

Enemies: Unknown

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: U.K.

First Appearance: 2000 A.D. #95-99

Powers/Abilities: The computer thought Harry was a F-20 fighter jet, allowing him heightened cognitive abilities and reflexes.

History: Angel was a fighter pilot who had his plane's flight computer grafted to his arm following a fire, granting him bizarre special powers.

Comments: Yeah, I know. This has got to be one of the all time dumbest excuses for why someone gained superhuman powers.

CLARIFICATIONS: Not to be confused with

  • Angel, vampire with a soul

  • Angel, Golden Age Timely hero

  • Angel Ace, Filipino heroine
