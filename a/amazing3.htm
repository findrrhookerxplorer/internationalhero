The Amazing Three

Membership: Blue Wizard, Tanya, Oakman

Purpose: To fight crime.

Affiliations: None.

Enemies: Volger

Base of Operations: U.K.

First Appearance:  Jackpot (I.P.C., 1979-80)

History: Don't have details. The three teenagers use the magic words "Rings of Zorr" to activate their transformations into their superhero forms.

Comments: Originally the Amazing Three fought crime in Newton City, in particular the arch criminal Volger.

Sue and Craig (Tanya and the Wizard) are brother and sister.

All three appeared as major supporting cast members in Zenith: Phase 3. Tanya was a casualty of the war against the Lliogor, being decapitated by a particularly vicious blow to the head. After the battle was won, Blue Wizard erected a monument in honour of all the fallen heroes, including his sister.

CLARIFICATIONS: Not to be confused with

  • Amazing Three, alien explorers disguised as everyday animals
