Angel Ace

Real Name: Angela Gale

Identity/Class: Superhuman, source of powers unknown

Occupation: Adventurer

Affiliations: Gregor (adopted father)

Enemies: Kai, the Sorcerer, SIVA

Known Relatives: Gregor (adopted father)

Aliases: None known

Base of Operations: The Philippines

First Appearance: Angel Ace (Alamat Comics)

Powers/Abilities: Able to fly, apparently by generating an anti-gravity field. She can also affect the mass and gravity of other objects, although at the moment she needs to touch them to do so. Slightly stronger than most people, with superhuman reflexes, speed, endurance and toughness. Although she isn't bulletproof, she can shrug off blows that would knock most people out, and she heals rapidly. Angel has photographic reflexes, allowing her to swiftly learn new skills simply by seeing them demonstrated once. Her father  also trained her in a variety of martial arts including karate, kung fu, savate, muay thai, capeoria, ninjitsu, jujitsu and some secret special forces military styles. In combat things appear to slow down to Angel, until the rest of the world seems to be moving in freeze-frame.

History: Angel was an orphan adopted as a child by former spy and mercenary Gregor, aka the Wolf, who found her a prisoner in the Temple of Clouds in the Himalayas. She grew up into a beautiful young woman, although her father's enemies made the notion of settling down in one place virtually impossible. Once she reached adulthood she somehow gained the ability to fly. This power drew unwanted attention from many who thought to exploit the young woman, forcing her to become a fugitive.

Comments: Created by Marco Dimaano
