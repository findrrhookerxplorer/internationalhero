Aerobica

Real Name: Sally Rogers

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: Government agent

Affiliations: The Sisterhood, the American Defense Organisation's Last Line

Enemies: Unknown

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Unknown

First Appearance: Sinnamon #2 (Catfish Comics)

Powers/Abilities: Superhuman stamina. Trained in most form of hand-to-hand combat.

History: Sally Rogers wanted to be an FBI agent, and was training to become one when she was recruited by the American Defense Organization (A.D.O.). They experimented on her, changing the nature of her hemoglobin to grant her heightened endurance. Afterwards she became the superheroine Aerobica, a member of their Last Line team of heroes.

Comments: Thanks to Angelo Furlan for informing me of this character
