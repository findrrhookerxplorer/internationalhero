Albion

Real Name: Peter Hunter

Identity/Class: Human transformed by magic

Occupation: Teacher

Affiliations: The Knights of Pendragon

Enemies: The Bane

Known Relatives: None known

Aliases: None known

Base of Operations:

First Appearance: Knights of Pendragon Vol. 1 #10

Powers/Abilities: Albion can manipulate magic fire and sense evil.

History: Peter Hunter was a history teacher who gained the powers of a Pendragon (a supernatural force that chooses certain mortals for important acts) just prior to World War I. As Albion, Peter rallied the British troops again and again. After the war, the powers seemed to fade, leaving only youthfulness in their wake. Peter grew older at a slower rate than most but was careful to hide this fact from his friends. In recent years, the powers have been returned to Peter and he now recognizes that being Albion is an important part of his life. Though he is not the team's official leader, his words carry great weight within the group.
