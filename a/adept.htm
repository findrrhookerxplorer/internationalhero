Adept

Real Name: Jelene Anderson

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: Soldier

Affiliations: member of Strikeforce: Morituri

Enemies: The Horde

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Strikeforce: Morituri's Mag-Lev Train, constantly travelling across the continental U.S.A.

First Appearance: Strikeforce: Morituri (Marvel Comics)

Powers/Abilities: Like all members of the Morituri, Adept's strength and endurance are superhuman. Her specific Morituri power was that of analysis - she could naturally study and comprehend virtually anything, including complex alien technology, given a little time.

History: A deeply spiritual and committed Christian, Jelene Anderson volunteered for the Morituri process out of a sense of responsibility after discovering she was genetically compatible for the process. In spite of her hatred of violence, she felt that if she could help defend humanity against the Horde, the sacrifice was worth it. She eventually died when returning with the team from a space trip to the vicinity of Jupiter.

Comments: Created by Carl Potts.
