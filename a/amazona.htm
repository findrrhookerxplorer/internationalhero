Amazona

Real Name: Amazona

Identity/Class: Human subspecies

Occupation: Adventurer

Affiliations: Blake Manners

Enemies: Gangsters

Known Relatives:

Aliases: The Mighty Woman

Base of Operations: U.S.A.

First Appearance: Planet Comics #3 (Fiction House, May 1940)

Powers/Abilities: Superhuman strength, endurance and durability. Resistant to sub-zero temperatures.

History: Amazona and her people are the last survivors of a super race that perished during the last ice age. Only the hardiest now survive the rigours of their new home, living in the frozen north away from the rest of mankind. When their citadel was accidentally discovered by Blake Manners, sole survivor of a ill-fated expedition, they gave him shelter, and Amazona, who had become enamoured of Manners, accompanied him when he returned home.

Comments: Created by Wilson Locke.

Amazona only made a single appearance, but she predates that other Amazon, Wonder Woman, by more than a year.

CLARIFICATIONS: Not to be confused with

  • Amazon, leader of Force 7
