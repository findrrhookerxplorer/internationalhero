Askal

Real Name: Lucio

Identity/Class: Human mutant

Occupation: Unknown

Affiliations: Sandugo

Enemies: Vital

Known Relatives: Unnamed mother

Aliases: Alamid

Base of Operations: Manila, Philippines

First Appearance: Unknown

Powers/Abilities: Askal possesses attributes similar to that of a canine and a savage brutality to match.

History: Askal is a product of experiment in mixing human and canine dna carried out on his mother. Regarded as a freak of nature, he decided to protect the weak against oppression to gain the acceptance of society.
