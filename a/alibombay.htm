Ali Bombay

Real Name: Ali Bombay

Identity/Class: Human technology user

Occupation: Unknown

Affiliations: M.A.S.K.

Enemies: V.E.N.O.M.

Known Relatives: Unidentified father

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: M.A.S.K. Headquarters at Boulder Hill, but active around the world

Powers/Abilities: Ali drove the Bullet motorbike and wore the Vortex Mask which could generate and control hurricane force winds.

History: Ali was secretly a member of the crimefighting M.A.S.K. organisation.
