Alamid

Real Name: David

Identity/Class: Human, mutated by magic.

Occupation: Unknown

Affiliations: Cheska, Brando, original Alamid

Enemies: Payaso, Golda, Dr. Blackman

Known Relatives: None known

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Philippines

First Appearance: Alamid (movie)

Powers/Abilities: Unknown

History: Alamid (Lito Lapid), a supernatural being who dwells among men, asks the gods to let him stay on earth a little longer. He wants to prepare humankind for his departure, and he wants to find someone who will take his place. His request is granted, and Alamid finds an infant floating on a river. He names him David (Dan Alvaro) and nurtures him to become a righteous man. When the right time comes, Alamid trains David, and soon after, he transfers to him all his powers. David is now the new Alamid. He goes after society’s evil elements, and in the process, he captures both the fancy and admiration of the people. But, concealed by his superhero costume, Alamid becomes an enigma to all....

Comments: Played by Lito Lapid (the elder Alamid) and Dan Alvaro (the younger).
