Abraxas

Real Name: Daniel Richmond

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: Unrevealed

Affiliations: None

Enemies: Burt Josler

Known Relatives: Unidentified wife

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Canada

First Appearance: Gamut #3 (1976)

Powers/Abilities: Able to generate bolts of cosmic energy, or opaque energy shields. Able to telepathically read minds, relay images, or enter others' minds to destroy the evil within.

History: "His name is Daniel Richmond. His sanity hangs by a thread which may snap at any moment...for he has beheld scenes beyond human comprehension. His mind holds only thoughts of revenge. He was born of Earth, but given the power of the stars. He is of good and evil, for he is: Abraxas!"

While being attacked by a drunken gang, Daniel Richmond was tranported away by extra-terrestrials for examination, leaving behind his defenceless wife. The aliens found in Daniel's mind a grim determination to return home and exact revenge on those who had attacked him, and so imbued him with cosmic powers to facilitate this.

Comments: Created by Poliwko.

CLARIFICATIONS: None.
