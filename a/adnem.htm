Admiral Nemesis

Real Name: Unknown

Identity/Class: Normal human

Occupation: Pirate

Affiliations: His crew

Enemies: Aquavenger

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: The seas and oceans of the world, in particular the Caribbean

First Appearance: Pow Annual 1971

Powers/Abilities: No innate powers, but Nemesis commands a high tech submarine called the Black Ray, and has the stereotypical master criminal pets, sharks and a giant squid, which he can throw heroes to.

History: Admiral Nemesis is a high-tech pirate and buccaneer. His reputation is such that Aquavenger instantly knows who he is the second he spots his infamous ship, the Black Ray. But despite the Admiral's fearsome reputation, his first encounter with the heroic Aquavenger ends with his capture.

(Aquavenger, Pow Annual 1972.) Bob Shane is sailing his fishing vessel off the New England seaboard when it is boarded by frogmen who warn him and his shipmate Shellback to leave the area. Once they are gone, Bob transforms into Aquavenger and follows them back to their base, which turns out to be the submarine Black Ray, "the Dark Ship of Admiral Nemesis himself". Spotted by more frogmen, Aquavenger is captured and taken aboard the sinister vessel, where Nemesis has him thrown into a tank containing a giant squid. By calling out his name a second time, Aquavenger renews his power and breaks free of the monster. He then threatens to flood the submarine and release Nemesis trained sharks amongst the crew. Nemesis and his men surrender.

Comments: He might have called himself "Admiral", but he clearly had no idea of ranks in the Navy, as his uniform has Commodore stripes - and so does that of his second in command.
