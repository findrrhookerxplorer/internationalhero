Allan Kämpe

Real Name: Allan Kämpe

Identity/Class: Normal human/Technology user

Occupation: Pilot (originally in the Swedish air force)

Affiliations: Hjärntrusten ("the brain trust")

Enemies: Harakoso

Known Relatives: Eva Kämpe (born Larsen), wife

Aliases: Unknown

Base of Operations: Secret base close to the North Pole

First Appearance: "Veckans Serier" issue 22-42 (1942)

Powers/Abilities: Experienced pilot

History: Forced to crash land near the North Pole when their plane suffers a fuel leak, aviators Allan and Eva Kämpe are rescued by the Hjärntrusten (Brain Trust), a society dedicated to solving the world's problems through advanced technology, and stopping the villains who would use such technology to mankind's detriment. Allan and Eva soon became the Hjärntrusten's best operatives, facing a variety of high tech criminals.

Comments: Created, written and drawn by Eugen Semitjov (1923-1987). The character first appeared in 1943 and was published weekly (1943-47) and daily (1945-57). After WW2 the comic was translated and published in Norway, Denmark, Finland, Argentina and Germany.
