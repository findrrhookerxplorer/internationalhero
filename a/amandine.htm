Amandine Faucon

Real Name: Amandine Faucon

Identity/Class: Human magic user

Occupation: Student, adventurer

Affiliations: Chai (raccoon familiar), Beowulf, Louis Bigsky

Enemies: Medea

Known Relatives: Medea (mother), unnamed aunt or uncle (leader of the Order of the Blood), unnamed father

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Unknown

First Appearance: The Grimoire #1 (Speakeasy Comics, 2005)

Powers/Abilities: Unspecified magical abilities

History: The Grimoire contains spells and arcane knowledge of immense power and danger. When it falls into the hands of Amandine Faucon, the daughter of a powerful and evil witch, it is up to the girl to keep it from the clutches of both her mother and all the others who would exploit what is within it for nefarious purposes.

Comments: Created by Sebastien Caisse and Djief.

CLARIFICATIONS: None
