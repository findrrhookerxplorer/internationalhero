American Wasteland

Created by R.D. Hall and Mark Kidwell, and published by Arcana Studios.

American Wasteland tells the story of trucker Cletus McCoy as he attempts to make it home to save his mother after America succumbs to a vampire apocalypse, assisted by a youth known as Sumbitch, and opposed by the vampires and their leader, Johnny Copperhead.
