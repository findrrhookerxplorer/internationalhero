Alpha Major

Real Name: Unknown

Identity/Class: Unknown

Occupation: Unknown

Affiliations: Hero Scouts (Lasso Lass, Genie Boy, Cry Baby, Ricky (a.k.a. Beta Boy)), Super-Amazing Man, Justice Five

Enemies: Diesel, General Gorilla

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: Megalotropolis

First Appearance: Hero@Large #1 (Speakeasy Comics, August 2005)

Powers/Abilities: Unknown

History: Alpha Major was the greatest hero of Megalotropolis until youthful upstart Diesel stole his spotlight. Dropped by his agent Barry Finkelstein at Super Talent Management, kicked out of the Justice Five, he became a down and out, until a young boy, Ricky, convinced him to return to the game, this time as the instructor of the Hero Scouts.

Comments: Created by Erick Hogan, Jeremy Treece and Sean "Cheeks" Galloway.
