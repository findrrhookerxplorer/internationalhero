Abbadon

Real Name: Unknown

Identity/Class: Human mutate

Occupation: Supervillain, formerly scientist

Affiliations: Black Day (leader), Typhon

Enemies: The Watch

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: Angel of the Abyss

Base of Operations: A complex hidden beneath the Nevada Desert

First Appearance: mentioned The Watch Volume 1, #1 (Phosphorescent Comics, March 2000)

partially seen The Watch Volume 1, #2 (Phosphorescent Comics, September 2000)

fully shown The Watch Volume 2, #2 (Phosphorescent Comics, November 2001)

Powers/Abilities: Abbadon's powers are largely unspecified but nonetheless believed to be quite extensive. He seems to be immune to most forms of attack and able to block the superhuman powers of others.

History: Abbadon was one of the scientists who first experimented with creating superhumans. He learned that the sudden appearance of superhumans on Earth was down to an alien species who view mankind as food, and superhumans as "ripe". Giving himself over to these beings, he allowed himself to be transformed into their instrument, charged with furthering this plan to prepare man as a source of sustenance for these beings.  Now he is one of the most powerful superhumans himself, and master of Black Day, a criminal organisation.

Comments: Created by Christian Read and Andrew Phillips

CLARIFICATIONS: None
