Ace of Space

Real Name: Ace Egan

Identity/Class: Human technology user

Occupation: Member of US Army Air Corps

Affiliations: None

Enemies: Slogons (evil aliens), Boris (spy), Speed Inc.

Known Relatives: None

Aliases: None

Base of Operations: USA

First Appearance: Feature Comics #38 (Quality, November 1940)

Powers/Abilities: When he dons his belt of power, he grows to become a 9 foot tall giant. He flies an alien rocket ship which can be turned invisible by touching a button on his belt.

History: Discovering a downed UFO and its dying passenger Jennings, Ace Egan salvaged its alien technology to battle spies and criminals; he later enlisted in the Air Corps.

Comments: Created by Weston Taylor and Harry Campbell.

Thanks to Carycomic for the images.

CLARIFICATIONS: Not to be confused with

  • The Ace, Canadian hero

  • Ace Hart, 1940's British superhero

  • Ace Lightning, CGI Hero

  • Ace McCloud, Centurions

  • Ace of Diamonds, Lone Star heroine

  • Ace of Wands, 1960's British television magician and crime buster

  • Ace Rimmer, from Red Dwarf: What a guy!

  • "Ace" Watson, Alpha Corps
